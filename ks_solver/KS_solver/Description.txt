This folder contains an implementation of the Probabilistic Differential Equation Solver (PODES) for the Kuramoto-Sivashinsky PDE. More information about PODES is presented in the paper Bayesian Uncertainty Quantification for Differential Equations, by O.A. Chkrebtii, D.A. Campbell, M.A. Girolami, B. Calderhead.
